Streamlit allows you to organize your app using layouts and containers

1. Columns
  col1, col2 = st.columns(2)
  with col1:
      st.header("Column 1")
      st.write("Hello")
  with col2:
      st.header("Column 2")
      st.write("World")

2. Expander
  with st.expander("See explanation"):
      st.write("Here you could put in some very cool stuff.")
